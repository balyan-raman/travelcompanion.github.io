About Us

Welcome to My Travel Companion – your one stop solution for travel to India. We specialize in authentic and rustic local experiences of India which are bespoke, off – beat , truly delightful and engaging for any traveler. Unlike traditional tour operators, we do this by buddying up with you during your journey and being a trusted friend and guide, helping you curate your travel itinerary at every step in a manner that allows you the freedom to explore your destination and at the same time, offers you the comfort of a trusted friend in a foreign land.

We invite you to a truly invigorating Indian experience – a journey that will awaken your spirits and leave you completely refreshed and rejuvenated.

Special Experiences

We are known for the special experiences we offer. These experiences can be made available under each of our services. So whether you are an international tourist or a domestic one, a solo traveler or travelling with family, an individual or a corporate or a school, we can organize these wonderfully unique, rustic and authentic Indian experiences for you and make your trip a memorable one.

Select from our list of experiences –

Rustic India

A day trip to a village in North India will give you a feel of the real India. This is an opportunity to immerse in the local culture and make heartwarming connections with the local families.
Here’s what Rustic India has in store for you –

  • A visit to the fields and an opportunity to try your hands at farming
  • Milking the cows and buffaloes, feeding them etc.
  • Conversations and meals with a local Indian family
  • Tractor & Bullock Cart rides

Spread Joy through Sharing

One day in your entire itinerary can be dedicated to spreading joy and bringing a smile on the faces of the lesser privileged people who also co – exist with us on this planet.
This includes one of more of the following -

  • A visit and spending time at a local orphanage or senior citizens home (as per your choice)
  • Meeting people on the street, connecting with them and lending clothes, money or just anything that they can use
  • Visiting a cowshed and feeding the cows

Travel Diaries

A service that converts any trip into a memorable experience to cherish forever. Travel Diaries provides a professional photographer who will accompany you to select destinations to capture memorable moments for you. Whether you are travelling with family or whether it is your annual corporate off – site, we could take some really awesome shots of all the fun and crazy things that happen and bring your entire trip to life.

Wellness Programs

Wellness programs cater to the mind, body and soul. They are aimed at rejuvenating the self and enabling you to re – connect with yourself at a deeper level. The wellness program can be a few hours session or a full – fledged 3 day intensive program.
Our Wellness Programs include workshops on –

  • Yoga
  • Meditation
  • Stress Management & Being Positive
  • Mindfulness
  • Mindful & Healthy Eating
